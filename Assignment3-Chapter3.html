Muffin Recipe

A basic recipe. You can add pretty much anything to it.

Ingrdients

  • 2 eggs
  • 20g caster sugar
  • 250ml milk
  • 125ml vegetable oil
  • 400g plain flour
  • 3tsp baking powder
  • 1tsp salt

Method

  1. Pre heat oven to Gas Mark 6 or a medium temperature.Line muffin tray with paper muffin cases.
  2. Beat the eggs in a mixing bowl and then mix the oil and milk.dd the sugar nd whisk until dissolved.
  3. Sift in the dry ingredient:flour,baking powder and salt and mix until smooth.nB:Add any extra igredients now eg.chocolate chips/fruis.
  4. Fill the muffin cases TWO THIRFS full(this is importanr for the perfect size mussins).
  5. Leave to cool(or enjoy warm).
